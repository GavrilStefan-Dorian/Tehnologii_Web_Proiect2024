Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to questions you may have. Can't find what you're looking for? Reach out to us at Contact

Account

How do I create an account?
To create an account, click on the Sign Up button over at Member Login and follow the instructions.
How do I reset my password?
You can reset your password by clicking on the Forgot Password link on the login page.
Can I change my username?
Unfortunately, username changes are not supported at this time.
How do I delete my account?
To delete your account, please contact customer support.

Book Reviews

How can I submit a book review?
To submit a book review, navigate to the book's page and click on the "Write a Review" button.
Can I edit or delete my book reviews?
Yes, you can edit or delete your book reviews from your profile page.
How do I report inappropriate content?
If you encounter any inappropriate content, please use the Contact Us form to notify our moderators.
Can I view other users' book reviews?
Yes, you can browse book reviews submitted by other users on the book's page.
